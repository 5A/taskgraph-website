Private and Secure

The program is self-hosted. Everything is stored in your own device and your own server. Industrial-standard encrytion and authentication are employed to protect your data.

Tasks in a Graph

All your to-do tasks in a project are organized into one or more directed graphs, allowing the program to analyze dependencies and determine what to focus on now.

Free and Open

The program is open source and free to use or modify. Standard formats and open protocols are used to store and transmit your data. Move in or move out easily at any time.
